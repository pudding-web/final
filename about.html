About GreenTown

We’re a neighborhood-run garden that focuses on education, sustainability, and access—so more people can grow food confidently.

How we support different visitors

  1. Accessible layout mobility
    • Wide paths and clear signage
    • Seating near common work areas
    • Raised beds for easier reach
  2. Clear information readability
    • High-contrast text and focus indicators
    • Descriptive link text (no “click here”)
    • Captions and transcripts for video
  3. Community support beginners
    • Mentor volunteers during events
    • Printable guides in large text
    • Tool checkout with quick demos

Our values

Accessibility

We design both the garden and the website so more people can participate.

Education

We share practical skills: soil care, watering, composting, and harvest planning.

Sustainability

We support pollinators, save water, and build healthy soil.
