About our Community

We’re a Community that focuses on education, learning, and access so more people can draw confidently.

How we support different visitors

  1. Accessible layout mobility
    • Wide paths and clear signage
    • Seating near common work areas
    • adjustable canvas for easier reach
  2. Clear information readability
    • High-contrast text and focus indicators
    • Descriptive link text (no “click here”)
    • Captions and transcripts for video
  3. Community support beginners
    • Mentor volunteers during events
    • Printable guides in large text
    • Tool checkout with quick demos

Our values

Accessibility

We design both the events and the website so more people can participate.

Education

We share practical skills: prespective, color theory, and stablization.

support

We support begginer, advanced, and artists that have little time to do art artists.
